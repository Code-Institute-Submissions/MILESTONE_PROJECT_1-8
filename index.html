Packages Available

Ruby

By far the most popular option is to have a Wedding DJ play after your band. This package caters for the more traditional Irish wedding. Set-up will be done early on the night, with your band. This avoids any interuption while your band is playing and also a seamless transition when they finish to keep you and your guests on the floor. As I am there early on the night, I am able to judge your guests and will have a great idea of what will work for you. It’s not simply a case of putting on a playlist and leaving it off. I will interact with your guests to ensure everyone is having the best night possible when you choose me to be your Wedding DJ.

Gold

This Wedding DJ package is becoming a lot more popular as some couples prefer to opt for a Wedding DJ for the full night instead of a band. This may be down to budget constraints, as having a Wedding DJ play for the full night is a lot less expensive than having a band or it may simply be by choice. I will get everyone up on the floor for your first dance and play all your favourite tunes and keep the party going all night long into the early hours of the morning. I will perform all the duties normally associated with the band, ie throwing the bouquet. I will also interact with your guests so I’m not just a jukebox. Playing time is 4 hours.

Diamond

This Wedding DJ package caters for your whole day event. Music will be provided 30 minutes prior to your ceremony beginning while your guests arrive. I will play the songs you want for the ceremony and then move on for your cocktail reception. If you wish you can then opt for the Ruby or Gold package. The choice is yours. While being a lot more cost effective this also means you only have to deal with one supplier for the whole of your wedding entertainment. It saves so much time in the planning leaving you to concentrate on the finer details of your perfect day. This is also ideal for smaller weddings and smaller venues where space can be limited.
